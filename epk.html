Electronic Press Kit

The Alley Kats — Wyoming reggae

Uplifting Wyoming reggae: love lost, redemption, and massive beach parties — with a sound the frontman calls medicine.

Bio

Multi-talented Wyoming reggae artist and lead singer Austin Taylor takes pride in being genuine to others and is a class act. The frontman views this unique sound as a form of medicine.

The Alley Kats, founded by Austin Taylor and Connor Crosby, is an uplifting band who sings songs about love lost, redemption and massive beach parties. Their album Road Less Traveled is available on streaming platforms and vinyl.

Lineup

  • Lead singer / Guitar: Austin Taylor
  • Bass: Bryce Taylor
  • Drums: Karrick Clifford
  • Keyboard: Amanda Baker
  • Lead Guitar: Anthony Schuman

Music & releases
