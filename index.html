About Us

Multi-talented Wyoming Reggae artist and Lead singer Austin Taylor takes pride in being genuine to others and is a class act. The frontman views this unique sound as a form of medicine.

The Alley Kats, founded by Austin Taylor and Connor Crosby, is an uplifting band who sings songs about love lost, redemption and massive beach parties. Their new album “ROAD LESS TRAVELED” is available now on all streaming platforms as well as Vinyl Record.

  • Lead singer / Guitar: Austin Taylor
  • Bass: Bryce Taylor
  • Drums: Karrick Clifford
  • Keyboard: Amanda Baker
  • Lead Guitar: Anthony Schuman

Welcome to the official home of The Alley Kats. Catch us at a show, browse our gallery, or get in touch for bookings.
